What is EduNotify?

EduNotify is a smart notification system designed to improve communication in schools and universities. With just one tap, school announcements, class schedules, and urgent alerts are delivered instantly.

Product Features

  • Real-time push notifications
  • Customized alerts for departments, year levels, and classes
  • Emergency alerts (power interruptions, sudden cancellations, etc.)
  • Weekly planner and calendar sync
  • Feedback system (emoji or short reply reactions)

Pricing Packages

  • Basic (Free) – One group, limited notifications
  • Standard (₱249/month) – Up to 10 groups, basic analytics
  • Premium (₱399/month) – Unlimited groups, full features + technical support

Benefits

  • Fast communication: Avoid the delays of physical bulletin boards.
  • Organized messaging: No more spammy group chats!
  • Student-focused: Designed with the modern student’s needs in mind.
  • Data protection: All data is encrypted and secure.
